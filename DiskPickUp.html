Reflection

Procedural Abstraction:
I implimented procedural abstraction by creating one main function and two helper functions. I did this to make my code more robust and less repetative. My two helper functions are: GotoNextLane() and drivePick_color(color). GotoNextLane() is called after all the disks of a specific color have been brought back to the goal, and it instructs the robot to move to the next goal. The second --drivePick_color(color)-- contains all the instructions on how to move and pick up the disks. This function is called nine times: three for green, three for blue, and three for red.

Logical Concepts:
I used logical operators throught the code. One example of this is:

while not distance.get_distance(MM) < 200:
drivetrain.drive(FORWARD)
wait(5, MSEC)

Here the logical concept is the "while not". This line prohibits the other lines from executing once the requirements after the not are met.
